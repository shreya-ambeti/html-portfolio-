The Best Movies According to Shreya

My top 3 Horror movies of all time

Evil Dead

A group of friends staying in a remote cabin accidentally awakens demonic forces when they read from the Book of the Dead, leading to a night of intense and gory supernatural horror.

The Conjuring:

Based on real-life paranormal investigations, Ed and Lorraine Warren confront malevolent spirits as they help a family plagued by supernatural phenomena, delivering a chilling and atmospheric supernatural horror experience.

The Nun:

Set in "The Conjuring" universe, "The Nun" explores the origins of a malevolent demonic entity as a priest and a novitiate uncover dark secrets while confronting unholy forces in a haunted Romanian abbey.
